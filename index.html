COMMONLY USED IN EVENTS

ROSES

Roses are timeless and come in various colors, each symbolizing different emotions. Red roses signify love and passion, making them a popular choice for weddings. White roses represent purity and innocence, often used in weddings to symbolize new beginnings.

Roses are classic symbols of love and romance, making them a popular choice for weddings and anniversaries. They come in various colors, each conveying a different sentiment. Red roses symbolize deep love, while white roses represent purity and innocence.

Roses are often used in bouquets, centerpieces, and as decorative elements for wedding cakes.




TULIPS

Tulips come in a variety of colors, and each hue carries a different meaning. Red tulips represent true love, while white tulips symbolize forgiveness. These versatile flowers can be incorporated into various wedding arrangements.

Tulips are known for their vibrant colors and sleek, modern look. They symbolize perfect love and are associated with the arrival of spring. Colors like red and white are popular choices for weddings.

Tulips are often used in various floral arrangements, from bouquets to table centerpieces.




ORCHIDS

Orchids are exotic and elegant, often chosen for their luxurious appearance. They symbolize love, beauty, and strength. Orchids add a touch of sophistication to wedding decor and bouquets.

Orchids exude exotic beauty and elegance. They symbolize love, beauty, and strength. With a variety of shapes and colors, orchids add a touch of sophistication to any arrangement.

Orchids are often featured in corsages, boutonnieres, and as accents in bridal bouquets.
